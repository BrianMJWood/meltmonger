May 27th, 2021: First Contact

Today was the first of many brief encounters I expect to have with Meltmongers. I walked by on accident.. or at least, I think it was an accident. I cannot rule out the possibility of some unseen hand guiding my path. Whether that hand is benevolent, malicious or whether it exists at all remains to be seen.


The door was closed. Perhaps the 'business' was simply closed for the day. Perhaps it was a symbol of the much deeper, darker door that is shut to me. I don't intend for it to remain shut much longer.
